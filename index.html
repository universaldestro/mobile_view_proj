Dutch baby pancake

Recipe Ingredients – Just write Recipe Ingredients

  • ½ cup flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ⅛ teaspoon nutmeg
  • 3 eggs
  • ⅔ cup milk
  • ¼ oil

Recipe Steps – Just write Recipe Steps

preheat oven to 425 degrees
Put pan in oven with the oil
Mix dry ingredients.
Mix eggs and milk
Mix all together
Let it sit for ten minutes
Put in oven for 14 minutes
Enjoy
